ABOUT MVGR

AUTONOMOUS COLLEGE 
    MVGR COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING - AN EPICENTER OF ACADEMIC EXCELLENCE
    The institute is situated amidst lush green flora and fauna that enhances 
    the instinct of learning incognito in the engineering minds. It is highly 
    committed to promote technocrats to their true values holistically being 
    imbibed with social responsibility and technical know-how in line with the 
    ethos of global service by providing all possible platforms to experience innovation, 
    industry-institute collaboration, outstanding academic & infrastructure resources. 
    With the eco-friendly water harvesting systems, solar systems, medicinal plantation etc. 
    the institution has a sustainable environment.
Campus (acres) : 60 Built up area (sft) : 700000 Laboratories : 83 Library Area (sft) : 12000

The Institution is listed under 2 (F), 12 (B) of UGC Act), Accredited by NBA of AICTE, Accredited by NAAC of UGC with “ A ” grade Approved by AICTE - New Delhi & Permanently affiliated to JNTU Kakinada

Vision

  • MVGR College of Engineering strives to become a center par excellence for technical education where aspiring students can be transformed into skilled & well rounded professionals with strong understanding of fundamentals, a flair for responsible innovation in engineering practical solutions applying the fundamentals, & confidence & poise to meet the challenges in their chosen professional spheres

Mission

The management believes imparting quality education in an atmosphere that motivates learning as a social obligation which we owe to the students, their parents/guardians and society at large and hence the effort is to leave no stone unturned in providing the same with all sincerity. Towards that end, the management believes special focus has to be on the following areas and provide them

  • Staff with high quality experience and facilitate life long learning opportunities in the field of industry and research advancements
  • A well stream-lined teaching learning process that is continuously assessed for effectiveness and fine-tuned for improvement
  • State-of-the-art lab and general infrastructure that gives students the necessary tools and means to enhance their knowledge and understanding
  • Centralized department focused on improving placement opportunities for our students directly on campus and coordinating the training programs for students to complement the curriculum and enhance their career opportunities
